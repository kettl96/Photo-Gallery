Miami

Miami located in Miami-Dade County in southeastern Florida. With a population of 467,963 , it is the second-most populous city in Florida, and 44th-most populous city in the United States. Miami is the core of the nation's eighth-largest metropolitan area with 6,138,333 people. The city has the third-largest skyline in the U.S. with over 300 high-rises, 58 of which exceed 491 ft (150 m).
